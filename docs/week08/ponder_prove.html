CSE 382: Week 08

Ponder and Prove

Tasks

Task 1

Write code in your language that implements the BST pseudocode functions found in the reading. Store significant amounts of data in your BST and ensure that it is behaving in accordance with the design as expressed in the pseudocode.

Task 2

Write code in your language that implements the Red-Black Tree pseudocode functions found in the reading. Store significant amounts of data in your BST and ensure that it is behaving in accordance with the design as expressed in the pseudocode.

Review

After completing the previous tasks:

  1. Review one of the many possible solutions for this task,
  2. Compare and contrast your solutions with the possible solutions, and
  3. Make note of any improvements you could make for future tasks.
