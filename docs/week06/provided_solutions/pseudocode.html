Function Declarations and Definitions

ID

\[\begin{align*} id\leftarrow [[],[]]. \end{align*} \]

In these definitions, \(\ell_1\) and \(\ell_2\) are of type List and \(a\) and \(b\) can be of the same or different types..

Append

\[\begin{align*} append\ &::\ [[a],[b]]\rightarrow [[a],[b]]\\ \\ append\ &::\ [\ell_1,\ell_2]\rightarrow\\ &[\ell_1\doubleplus \ell_2,[]]. \end{align*} \]

Intersection

\[\begin{align*} intersection\ &::\ [[a],[b]]\rightarrow [[a],[b]]\\ \\ intersection\ &::\ [\ell_1,\ell_2]\rightarrow\\ &[[e\mid e\in \ell_1\ and\ e\in \ell_2],[]]. \end{align*} \]

Difference

\[\begin{align*} difference\ &::\ [[a],[b]]\rightarrow [[a],[b]]\\ \\ difference\ &::\ [\ell_1,\ell_2]\rightarrow\\ &[[e\mid e\in \ell_1\ and\ e\not\in\ell_2],[]]. \end{align*} \]

Symmetric Difference

\[\begin{align*} symmetric\_difference\ &::\ [[a],[b]]\rightarrow [[a],[b]]\\ \\ symmetric\_difference\ &::\ [\ell_1,\ell_2]\rightarrow\\ &[[e\mid e\not\in \ell_1\ or\ e\not\in\ell_2],[]]. \end{align*} \]
